Intro To DCS

All the buttons on the menu do exactly what they say they will do. For mission and campain modes you will need to provide your own files.
To practice combat I like to choose Instant Action-->Target Practice and just restart the level when needed.

It is very important to learn all the important key bindings, or else you should get used to checking the bindings menu.

DCS world can be a pretty demanding game so make sure to adjust the graphical setting so you can reach a frame rate that is greater than your monitor's refresh rate. I have a Ryzen 1600, a GTX 1050TI and a 7200PRM HDD and get around 90 FPS on decent settings.

To practice regular flying Create Fast Mission then changing the the settings to your needs.
