Flying a plane

Flying a plane in realistic and arcade is pretty easy. The basic controls are just moving the {MOUSE} around in the direction where you want to go. It is similar to tanks in that the plane wont instantly go to where you are pointed but will takes some time to get there.

Where you are pointing
Where your plane is pointing

A plane will usually have these types of weapons

  1. Cannons
  2. Machine Guns
  3. Bombs/Torpedos

To toggle flaps press {F}. To toggle landing gear press {G}. To toggle airbrake press {B}. These will only toggle if they are available.

If you want to actually control your plane better you can use {MOUSEWHEEL UP} and {MOUSEWHEEL DOWN} to throttle up and down. {A} and {D} will roll left and right.
