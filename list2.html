How to fly a plane

For this guide I will be using the default bindings on keyboard/mouse. You can use a controller or a joystick, but I don't becase my controller is around 20 years old, and I don't have a joystick.

To start up the plane so you can start moving use {RIGHT SHIFT + L} for electricity then {RIGHT SHIFT + HOME} to start engines. After that use {RIGHT CONTROL + C} to close the cockpit, {LEFT SHIFT + F} to have flaps in landing/takeoff position. Wait for the green lights that I zoom into to turn off to use {PGUP/PGDOWN} to increase and decrease throttle. It should be around 4 on indicator thingy both above and below

Basic controls are {Z} or {R} for using the rudder to turn left and right. To bank left and right use {ARROWKEY LEFT} or {ARROWKEY RIGHT}. To pitch up and down use {ARROWKEY UP} or {ARROWKEY LEFT}. To slow your movement you can use {B} to toggle the airbrake.

Try to get at LEAST 170kmph

To get authentic experince like I had turn on Native language, which changes everything to Russian.
